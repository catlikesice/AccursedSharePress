About Us

The Accursed Share Press is a Tbilisi-based independent publisher focused on critical works exploring violence and sexuality in political, theological, philosophical, and artistic dimensions. We publish monographs, translations, and collaborative projects engaging with thinkers like Georges Sorel, Walter Benjamin, Max Weber, Georges Bataille, others as well as their contemporary interlocutors.
